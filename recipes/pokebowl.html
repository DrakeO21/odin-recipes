Ahi Tuna Poke Bowl

Description

Ahi Tuna Poke bowls are a staple delicacy in the Hawaiian culture. They were later introduced internationally in 1970's and 1980's and are now enjoyed around the world.

This delicious bowl consists of sushi rice, bluefin tuna, and an assortment of vegetable and dry toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sushi grade ahi tuna, cut into 3/4" cubes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 3 medium-sized carrots, shredded
  • 1 whole cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup edamame
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds, plus more for garnish
  • 2 cups brown, or white rice, whichever is your preference, cooked

Steps

  1. Cook the rice - Wash the rice under cold water until water turns clear and cook in a rice cooker according to the manufacturer's instructions
  2. Marinate the tuna - In a medium-sized bowl combine ahi tuna, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, green onions, and sesame seeds
  3. Combine Ingredients - Add the cooked rice, tuna, and desired toppings to each bowl. Serve immediately or refrigerate covered up for up to 2 hours before serving.

Acai Bowl

Description

Following the theme of hawaiian cuisine, the acai bowl is a creation of blended acai berries, granola, and an assortment of toppings, with a sweet drizzle to top it off.

I would say these sweet bowls are best enjoyed on a summer day, laying at the beach but they can be enjoyed throughout the entire year, no matter the weather.

Acai Berry Bowl Ingredients

  • 2 Acai Superfruit Packs
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana frozen, preferably
  • 1/4-1/2 cup apple juice (can subsitute coconut water or almond milk)
  • 2 tablespoons protein powder (preferred flavor) (optional)
  • ice (optional)

Acai Bowl Toppings

  • Almond Butter
  • Cacoa Nibs or Shaved Dark Chocolate
  • Unsweetened Coconut Flakes
  • Bananas
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Mangoes
  • Pineapple
  • Granola
  • Honey or Agave

Steps

  1. Blend acai, blueberries, strawberries, banana, protein powder of your choice, and apple juice(or other substitute).
  2. If you are using frozen fruit, you will need to use 1/2 cup of apple juice, to help the blender break it down. If you are using fresh fruit, 1/4 cup of apple juice will suffice.
  3. Take contents out of the blender and put them into a serving bowl of your choice.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings. We gave you a list of some popular ones as a guide but you are welcome to use whatever you'd like.
  5. Finally, drizzle with honey(or agave) and enjoy!
